Water softener · New service

Hard water is common across South Delhi — here's the fix

A lot of Mehrauli, Chattarpur and Aya Nagar homes run on borewell or tanker supply, which usually carries more dissolved calcium and magnesium than municipal lines. Left untreated, that hardness shows up as scale on geysers, taps, and — over time — extra strain on your RO membrane.

We now install and service dedicated water softeners, sized to your household and fitted ahead of your existing RO unit.

  • Longer life for geysers, taps and RO membranes
  • Softer skin & hair from bathing water
  • Fits ahead of your current RO — no need to replace it
  • Salt refill & resin service plans available

FAQ — RO & softener service in Mehrauli

Do you provide RO service in Mehrauli, Delhi?

Yes. We're based in Mehrauli and cover Mehrauli plus nearby South Delhi localities including Chattarpur, Saket, Vasant Kunj and Aya Nagar for RO and softener service.

Is Delhi's water hard, and do I need a softener?

Many homes here run on borewell or tanker water, which tends to be harder than municipal supply. A softener fitted ahead of your RO reduces scaling on pipes, geysers and the membrane itself.

What does RO service cost in Mehrauli?

Cost depends on the issue and parts needed. Share your requirement through the booking form or call us for a transparent quote before any work begins.

Do you offer an Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)?

Yes — our AMC plans cover scheduled filter changes, membrane checks and priority repair visits.

How fast can a technician reach me in Mehrauli or nearby areas?

Most requests in the Mehrauli area are scheduled same-day or next available slot; emergency repairs get priority.

Can you install a water softener alongside my existing RO?

Yes — softeners are usually fitted ahead of your existing RO system without needing to replace the RO unit itself.
